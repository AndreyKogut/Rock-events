Group «Red Hot Chili Peppers» in Kiev, February 19th, 2016 at the Sports Palace.

Legendary "Red Hot Chili Peppers" returned to Kiev with a big concert in the framework of a grand world tour 50th Anniversary World Tour 2015 / 2016.2015 - a jubilee year for the group "Red Hot Chili Peppers." The team, which can rightly be called the most popular rock - the team is 50 years old!

For half a century «Red Hot Chili Peppers" represent real quality rock music and stunning in its drive and energy concerts, invariably accompanied anshlagami.
