Red Hot Chili Peppers

The best concert in 2016. Tickets at face value by the organizer of the concert.

  • 12 MayMonday, 19:00

Group «Red Hot Chili Peppers» in Kiev, February 19th, 2016 at the Sports Palace.

Legendary "Red Hot Chili Peppers" returned to Kiev with a big concert in the framework of a grand world tour 50th Anniversary World Tour 2015 / 2016.2015 - a jubilee year for the group "Red Hot Chili Peppers." The team, which can rightly be called the most popular rock - the team is 50 years old!

For half a century «Red Hot Chili Peppers" represent real quality rock music and stunning in its drive and energy concerts, invariably accompanied anshlagami. Grup «Red Hot Chili Peppers» looks forward to meeting with the audience and invites mark the fiftieth anniversary of its history at a live concert on February 19 in Kiev Sports Palace in the framework of large-scale global turne.Kontsert «Red Hot Chili Peppers» - this is a real spectacle of emotions and the ability to once again hear the legendary hits. Crazy drive, an amazing show - all this is waiting for those who are going to attend a concert in Kiev.

Jubilee 2015 «Red Hot Chili Peppers» began with the presentation of the movie «Forever and a Day». The famous film director Katja von Garnier ("Bandidas", "East Wind") was accompanied by «Red Hot Chili Peppers» in their farewell tour «Final Sting-Tour», and 18 months captured unique footage - moments of communion between the members.
